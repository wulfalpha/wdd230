Membership Benefits

These are just some of the amazing benifits you could receive by joining the Utah Valley Chamber of Commerce.

Non Profit

A plan for Non Profit organizations. Allows them to get many of the benefits they need for free. Networking and Job Fair opportunities are just some examples.

Bronze

An affordable Tier for small businesses. Get the benifits of networking, job fairs, and more.

Silver

Mid-level Tier for small to medium businesses. Get access to Job Fairs, a liason with the local school districts and more.

Gold

Highest Tier. For large businesses or businesses looking for more exposure. Get access to all of the above and in addition to our heartfelt thanks, a spotlight on all our web pages.
