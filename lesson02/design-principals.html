WDD 230: Front End Design Principals

Harvard Art Museum:

Harvard Art Museum is an excellent example of the principle of whitespace and clean design. Their website is not cluttered nor is it completely blank. Instead, it makes clever use of white space to highlight what the place is about.

Google Search

Google's search site is a perfect example of Fitt's law. Using an excellent example of "magic pixels" it groups the search options in the center of the screen along with all the action buttons in the corners of the page.

Squarespace.com

Squarespace is a great example of visual hierarchy. The site is designed to draw your eyes to sign up and use their service to creat easy and responsive websites. The site draws the eyes to these important features.
